About The Awareness Times

At The Awareness Times our mission is to educate, inform and empower our readers by shedding light on pressing social issues, especially those that are often silenced, abused, fight injustice and inequality. We are committed to being the voice of the voiceless, promoting awareness, encouraging dialogue, and inspiring action. Through truthful reporting, impactful storytelling and community engagement, we aim to build a more informed and compassionate society. The Awareness Times is dedicated to raising awareness about all forms of abuse, physical, emotional, sexual and psychological. Our mission is to educate the public, break the silence surrounding abuse and support survivors by sharing their stories with empathy and dignity. We aim to foster a culture of accountability, prevention and healing through honest journalism, community outreach and advocacy for justice and change.
